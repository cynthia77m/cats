Cats

Cats were only recently domisticated, and have been part of the human household for a couple of thousand years. Unlike most animals cats choose to live with humans.

Feeding

Kittens should be feed around four times a day, while older cats should be feed around two to three times a day. If your cat is feral, it may spend a lot of time hiding, which means creating a feeding schedule may be tricky. Leave out dry food, and clean water, and give your cat plenty of space. If it feels safe it will come out to eat.

Maintaince

You should brush your cat once a day, and invest in a lint roller. If your cat is an outdoor cat, (strongly discouraged) you should also give your cat a bath at least once a month, and check for fleas. Click HERE for to find best hair brush for you feline friend.

Body Language

Swishing tail means leave me alone, it is the first warning sign a cat gives. After the tail switching depending on the cat it may either walk away, hiss or attack. If the body is arched it is scared.
